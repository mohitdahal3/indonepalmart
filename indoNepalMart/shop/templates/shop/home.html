How it works?

  1. Browse on Amazon

    Start your shopping journey by browsing through the extensive selection of products available on Amazon's website.

  2. Place Your Order

    Once you've found the perfect item, simply click "Place your Order" back in our website to proceed. Fill out the provided form with your name, email, phone number, address, product's pictures and the product's link.

  3. Connect with Us

    After submitting the form, you'll be redirected to our WhatsApp to directly message our team. Here, you can discuss your product selection, preferences, and any additional details.

  4. Confirm Your Order

    If you haven't messaged us on WhatsApp, don't worry! We'll reach out to you using the contact information provided in the form. Our goal is to ensure seamless communication and address any questions or concerns you may have.

  5. Enjoy Hassle-free Delivery

    Sit back and relax as we handle the rest! We'll work tirelessly to minimize costs and ensure prompt delivery of your chosen products to your doorstep. Whether through trusted shipping partners or our own delivery network, your satisfaction is our priority.
